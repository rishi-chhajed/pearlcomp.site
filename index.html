Pearls Computers



Pearls Computers is run by dedicated entrepreneurs who have established benchmarks in their field. Pearls is a leading IT and ITES organisation which is catering to the areas of software developement, Cash Management Services (CMS) and related logistics to prestigious telecom companies and banks. Established in 1991, Pearls Computers is headquartered at Indore, the commercial capital of central India. Armed with domain knowledge in the fields of Banking, Financial Services, Insurance (BFSI), telecom and software development, we have our priorities in line with customer needs and expectations and it is our continual endeavour to meet and surpass the same. As a part of forward integration we compliment banking services in Telecom Cash Management System and Services and have carved a formidable niche in volume and reputation (53 offices in 48 cities in four states).


The entire structure  primarily deals with three-four distinct divisions namely Pearls,  Little Monk, Oye24 and IndoreAI. Pearls Computers, as a parent company, delivers a wide range of  technological and non-technological operations. The company also has a  broad range of software and data-entry related solutions. Equipped with the latest technology in place and the best  combination of skill sets, Pearls’ handles inbound  and outbound voice processes as well as projects ranging from simple keying to complex data processing jobs. Prizm is one of our best product, which is becoming a major utility  for BFSI & Telco’s in India. The capability to process the  customer-dealer network transactions has made it an established brand.

Little Monk

This is what inspires us at a little monk restaurant in Indore. Oye24



Launched in 2015, Oye24 has since become an important name in the food-delivery sector of Indore, and many other cities in India.

Our goal has been to deliver pure vegetarian fare from our kitchen to people’s doorstep, at every and any hour of the day. Our menu is a delightful mix of North Indian and Chinese cuisine, wherein one can find dishes such as the mighty Biriyani, the humble Kadhai Paneer and the local favourite Dudh Sev sabzi.

We have successfully continued to achieve our primary goal with incredible results; over five lakh orders delivered, out of which one lakh were of our phenomenal Butter Khichdi. We pride ourselves over the stupendous growth of Oye24 and aim to incorporate the same dedication in #Instaगरम.
